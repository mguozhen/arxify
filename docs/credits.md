# Credit System

## Per-run costs

| Operation | Credits | Wall-clock | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| DeepCritique | 100 | ~3 min | Single MiroThinker call |
| SearchLiterature | 50 | ~1 min | Single MiroThinker call |
| Ideation (5 ideas × 3 reflections) | 500 | ~15 min | Each idea: 1 search + optional critique + finalize |
| Rank ideas | 200 | ~5 min | Single MiroThinker call across all ideas |
| Tournament (~20 ideas) | 800 | ~15 min | ~19 pairwise comparisons |
| Writeup (8-page paper) | 1500 | ~30 min | LaTeX rendering + multi-step Claude calls |
| Writeup (4-page workshop) | 800 | ~15 min | Shorter |

## Per-plan allowances

| Plan | $/mo | Credits/mo | Approx runs |
|---|---|---|---|
| Spark | $0 | 300 | 1 ideation, OR 3 critiques |
| Scholar | $39 | 5,000 | ~10 ideation + 5 tournaments + 1 writeup |
| Lab | $99 | 15,000 | ~30 ideation + 10 tournaments + 5 writeups + team usage |

## Hard limits (per plan, not credit-based)

| Plan | Active projects | Team seats | API access |
|---|---|---|---|
| Spark | 1 | 1 | ❌ |
| Scholar | Unlimited | 1 | ❌ |
| Lab | Unlimited | 3 | ✅ |

Extra seats on Lab: $20/seat/mo (5-seat min, 25K shared credits).

## Top-up packs (no commitment)

- 1,000 credits — $10
- 5,000 credits — $39
- 20,000 credits — $129

Top-ups don't expire. Plan credits roll over up to 2 months.
